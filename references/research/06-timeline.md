# Kris Jenner: Timeline of Crisis & Conversion (06-timeline.md)

Kris Jenner’s brand management is characterized by an immediate response to crisis, followed by a strategic "conversion" of that crisis into a business opportunity. Below is a timeline of major scandals, her response patterns, and the resulting business outcomes.

| Year | Scandal/Event | Response Pattern | Business Conversion/Outcome |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **2007** | **Kim's Sex Tape** | **Immediate.** Settled lawsuit within weeks; addressed tape in first episode of *KUWTK*. | Pitch and launch of *Keeping Up With The Kardashians* (KUWTK). |
| **2011** | **The 72-Day Marriage** | **Strategic.** Framed as a "heartbreaking mistake" on the show; handled divorce with media blackout. | The wedding generated **$18M** in sponsorships and TV rights. The divorce drama fueled ratings for spin-offs. |
| **2014-15** | **Kylie's Lip Filler** | **Delayed (1 Year).** Denied for months; admitted on *KUWTK* after maximum viral speculation. | Launched **Kylie Lip Kits**, sold out in <1 minute. Eventually grew into **Kylie Cosmetics** (billion-dollar brand). |
| **2013-17** | **Khloé's Marriage Collapse** | **Real-Time.** Documented divorce and Odom's health crisis; Khloé's fitness as "stress relief." | Trademarked **"Revenge Body,"** which became a best-selling book and E! reality series. |
| **2017** | **Kylie's Secret Pregnancy** | **Total Blackout (9 Mos).** Orchestrated complete silence; no confirmation to press. | Reveal YouTube video ("To Our Daughter") broke internet records. Proved direct control over narrative. |
| **2018-19** | **Kendall's Acne** | **Calculated (1 Year).** Waited exactly one year after Golden Globes mockery to address it. | Teased "vulnerable announcement" which was a multi-million dollar **Proactiv** partnership. |
| **2018-22** | **Tristan Thompson Cheating** | **Real-Time.** Captured family reactions immediately for the reality show. | Central plot for *KUWTK* Season 15 and *The Kardashians* Season 1. High ratings and audience empathy. |
| **2021** | **E! to Hulu Transition** | **Strategic Pivot.** Recognized staleness of cable TV; negotiated prestige streaming deal. | Shift to **Hulu's *The Kardashians*** allowed for faster turnaround and better integration with D2C brands. |
| **2022** | **Balenciaga Controversy** | **Mirroring Response.** Used strong emotive words ("outraged," "shaken") to align with public anger. | Positioned the family as fellow victims/concerned observers, insulating their individual brands. |

## Key Patterns in the Timeline
1.  **Immediate Alignment:** Response begins with a "war room" style family meeting to ensure a unified front.
2.  **The "10% Rule":** Jenner takes a 10% management fee on all resulting business conversions, ensuring her own empire grows with every family scandal.
3.  **Vertical Integration:** Use the reality show as a "fixed cost" marketing platform for every new business venture spawned from a crisis.
4.  **Attention as Currency:** Even negative press is treated as a traffic-driving event for their digital platforms and business ecosystem.
